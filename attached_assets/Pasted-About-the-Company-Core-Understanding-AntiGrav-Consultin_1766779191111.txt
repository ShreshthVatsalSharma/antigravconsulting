About the Company (Core Understanding)

Helps global businesses analyze the Indian market

Designs India market-entry strategies

Acts as a long-term strategic partner rather than a one-time advisor

AntiGrav positions itself as:

Strategic

Data-driven

Founder-led

India-focused but globally relevant
